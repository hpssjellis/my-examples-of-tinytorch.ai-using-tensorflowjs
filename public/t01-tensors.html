Basic Demonstration of TensorFlow.js Tensors


See the video here.



This page now demonstrates the core **Tensor** concepts from **TinyTorch Chapter 1**:
  1. **Creation** of Tensors (Scalars, 2D Arrays).
  2. **Element-Wise** Operations (Addition, Multiplication, Broadcasting).
  3. **Reshaping** (Flatten, Transpose).
  4. **Reduction** Operations (Sum, Mean).
  5. **Matrix Multiplication** (`tf.matMul`).
  6. **Memory Management** (`tf.tidy`).
